SSIA: ShadowFront

A future title in the SSIA Games franchise. Set within the SSIA Universe, ShadowFront follows the operations of the SSIA Fugitives Task Force (FGTF), a specialized unit dedicated to tracking and apprehending dangerous fugitives across Sierra Liudoxoya.
